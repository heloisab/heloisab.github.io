About me

My name is Antonio Carlos Luppi Junior, AKA Luppi. I am almost graduating in Electrical Engineering @ UFSC, at the city of Florianópolis - Brazil. Electronic Designer who comprehends that technology can be ludic, accessible, and inspiring.

Professionally, I believe that the in-time & simple solution is way better than the perfect-but-late solution, andI usually works on rapid prototyping and quick learning and iteration from failures to get things done. Bringing the sense of creativity in solving problems in unconventional ways. I also conceive that respect and listening are the keys for good teamwork and sinergy.

I'm member of Tarrafa HackerClube, a local Hackerspace in Florianópolis, and I am very enthusiastic of the Hacker culture. I have future plans to start some side projects concerning Art, Tech, and Education.
